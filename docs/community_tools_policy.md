# Community Tools & External Resources Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

This policy governs the "For You" section of the VIT-AP Student App, which showcases student-made tools, resources, and event links submitted by members of the VIT-AP University community.

---

## 1. About This Section

The "For You" section is a community-driven feature that allows VIT-AP students to discover and share helpful tools, resources, and event information created by fellow students. This platform aims to foster innovation and collaboration within the student community.

## 4. Submission Guidelines

### 4.1 Eligibility

To submit a tool or resource:

- You must be a VIT-AP University student or alumni
- The tool/resource must be relevant to VIT-AP students
- You must have the right to share the provided URL

### 4.2 Approval Process

- All submissions require manual approval before appearing in the app
- The developer reserves the right to reject any submission without explanation
- Approval does not create any ongoing obligation
- Approved items may be removed at any time

### 4.3 Prohibited Content

Submissions must NOT contain:

- Malicious software or security threats
- Inappropriate, offensive, or harmful content
- Misleading or fraudulent information
- Content that violates any laws or regulations
- Spam or commercial advertisements
- Content that infringes on intellectual property rights

## 5. Reporting Issues

If you encounter any issues with a listed tool or resource, including:

- Security concerns
